Porsche

The Porsche 911 has long been one of the purest sports cars on the planet, with unmistakable styling and ethereal performance. The 2020 Porsche 911 institutes an all-new generation—which Porsche codenames internally as 992—that includes a handsomely evolved design and meticulously engineered mechanicals. While the lineup hasn't been fully rounded out, the 911 is currently available as a sporty coupe or snazzy convertible (a.k.a. cabriolet in Porsche-speak). Porsche continues to sell remaining 2019 models such as the hard-core 911 Carrera T and the satisfying 911 Carrera GTS.
